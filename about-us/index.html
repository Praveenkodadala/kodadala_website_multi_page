Who We Are

India's Spices, Delivered to the World

Kodadala Trading Company is a Hyderabad-based export firm specialising in premium Indian spices and agricultural products. We bridge the gap between India's rich agricultural heritage and the global demand for authentic, high-quality spices.

With direct farm partnerships across major spice-growing regions — Guntur, Erode, Nizamabad, Gujarat, Rajasthan, and Kerala — we ensure every shipment meets the highest standards of quality, purity, and consistency.

Our team handles the complete export cycle: procurement, quality testing, packaging, documentation, and logistics — so our buyers can focus on their business while we take care of theirs.
